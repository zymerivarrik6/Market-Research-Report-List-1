<p><p>The dry air scrubber market is classified into two main types: 3-stage air scrubbers and 4-stage air scrubbers. </p><p>3-stage air scrubbers typically consist of a pre-filter, a chemical scrubber, and a final particulate filter, effectively removing particulate matter and harmful gases. </p><p>In contrast, 4-stage air scrubbers introduce an additional purification step, often incorporating advanced filtration technologies to enhance pollutant removal efficiency. This makes 4-stage units suitable for more demanding environments with stricter air quality regulations and higher contaminant levels.</p></p>
