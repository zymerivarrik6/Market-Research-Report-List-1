<p><strong>The Direct-acting Solenoid Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step-by-step direct-acting diaphragm structure</li><li>Direct acting piston structure</li><li>Direct motion diaphragm structure</li><li>Step-by-step piston structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The direct-acting solenoid valves market features several types, each with unique structures. The step-by-step direct-acting diaphragm structure utilizes a diaphragm that sequentially opens and closes under electromagnetic actuation, ensuring precise flow control. The direct-acting piston structure employs a piston that directly responds to solenoid activation, providing reliable sealing and quick response. The direct motion diaphragm structure operates with a diaphragm moving directly in response to solenoid input, offering consistent performance. The step-by-step piston structure utilizes a piston mechanism for staged flow control, enhancing operational efficiency. Each type caters to specific applications in fluid automation and process control.</p></p>
