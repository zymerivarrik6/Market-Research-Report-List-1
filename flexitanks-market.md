<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Flexitanks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multilayer Flexitanks</li><li>Monolayer Flexitanks</li><li>Bilayer Flexitanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexitank market consists of three main types: multilayer, monolayer, and bilayer flexitanks. Multilayer flexitanks feature multiple polymer layers, providing enhanced strength and protection against leaks and punctures, making them ideal for transporting sensitive liquids. Monolayer flexitanks are simpler, with a single layer that is cost-effective for less sensitive products. Bilayer flexitanks combine the benefits of both, offering a dual-layer design that balances durability and cost, suitable for a variety of liquid cargoes in bulk transportation.</p></p>
