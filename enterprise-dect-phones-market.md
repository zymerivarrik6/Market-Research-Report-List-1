<p><p>The Enterprise DECT Phones market is primarily segmented into SIP DECT and IP DECT categories. SIP DECT integrates Session Initiation Protocol, enabling seamless connectivity with VoIP systems for scalable and flexible communication. IP DECT, on the other hand, utilizes Internet Protocol for direct communication with IP-based networks, offering enhanced audio quality and features. Both types support wireless mobility in business environments, with SIP DECT focusing on compatibility with existing telephony systems, while IP DECT emphasizes integration with modern digital infrastructures.</p></p>
