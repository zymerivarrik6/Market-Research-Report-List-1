<p><p>The Glass Printing Ink market is primarily categorized into two types: oily glass ink and water glass ink. Oily glass ink is solvent-based, characterized by vibrant colors and durability, making it suitable for high-temperature applications and outdoor use. In contrast, water glass ink is eco-friendly, featuring a water-based formulation that offers lower emissions and easier cleanup. While oily inks excel in permanence and gloss, water inks are favored for sustainability and safety in various glass printing applications.</p></p>
