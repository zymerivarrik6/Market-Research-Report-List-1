<p><p>The Cantilever Waterjet Cutting Machine market is divided into two primary types: Pressurized Water and Abrasive Mixture. Pressurized Water cutting utilizes high-pressure water jets to slice through softer materials like foam and rubber, offering precision without the need for abrasives. In contrast, the Abrasive Mixture method combines water with abrasive particles, making it suitable for cutting harder materials such as metal and stone. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, emphasizing efficiency and versatility in cutting technologies.</p></p>
