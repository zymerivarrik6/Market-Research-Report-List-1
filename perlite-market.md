<p><strong>The Perlite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crude</li><li>Expanded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The perlite market can be divided into two main types: crude perlite and expanded perlite. Crude perlite refers to the naturally occurring volcanic glass that is mined and processed minimally, retaining its original form. Expanded perlite, on the other hand, is produced by heating crude perlite to high temperatures, causing it to expand into lightweight, porous granules. Expanded perlite is commonly used in construction, horticulture, and industrial applications due to its insulating properties and moisture retention capabilities.</p></p>
