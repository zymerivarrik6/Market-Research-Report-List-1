<p><p>Titania ceramic membranes are categorized into two main types: dense and porous membranes. Dense membranes are characterized by their impermeable nature, making them ideal for applications requiring selective ion transport and separation processes. In contrast, porous membranes feature interconnected pores that allow for the filtration and separation of particles and fluids, suitable for wastewater treatment and gas separation. Each type harnesses the unique properties of titania to enhance efficiency in various industrial applications, addressing different separation and filtration needs.</p></p>
