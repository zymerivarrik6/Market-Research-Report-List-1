<p><p>The carbon steel market is divided into three main types based on carbon content: low carbon steel, medium carbon steel, and high carbon steel. Low carbon steel, containing up to 0.3% carbon, is malleable and used in automotive and construction applications. Medium carbon steel, with 0.3% to 0.6% carbon, balances strength and ductility, ideal for manufacturing machinery and automotive components. High carbon steel, with over 0.6% carbon, offers high hardness and strength, making it suitable for tools and cutting applications.</p></p>
