<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shipboard incineration market can be categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical incinerators. Horizontal incinerators feature a long cylindrical design, allowing for efficient waste loading and combustion, while promoting easy maintenance and ash removal. In contrast, vertical incinerators have a compact design, optimizing space onboard vessels and improving thermal efficiency. Both types are crucial for managing waste disposal at sea, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and minimizing the ecological impact of marine operations.</p></p>
