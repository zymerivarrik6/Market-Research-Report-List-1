<p><p>The Low Voltage AC-DC LED Driver market comprises several types, including Buck Drivers, Boost Drivers, and Multi-channel Drivers. Buck Drivers efficiently lower voltage from a higher input level to power LEDs, making them ideal for applications requiring reduced voltage. Boost Drivers, conversely, step up lower voltage to meet LED requirements, suitable for battery-powered devices. Multi-channel Drivers provide control and power to multiple LED strings simultaneously, enabling versatile lighting solutions for varied applications, enhancing energy efficiency and design flexibility.</p></p>
