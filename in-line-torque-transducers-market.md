<p><p>In-line torque transducers are essential for measuring torque in various applications. Static torque sensors are designed to measure torque at rest or in non-rotating conditions, making them suitable for applications like calibration and testing. In contrast, dynamic torque sensors are used for real-time measurements in rotating systems, capturing torques in machinery, motors, and automotive applications. Both types are crucial for ensuring accuracy and reliability in performance monitoring, helping to optimize operations and enhance safety across diverse industries.</p></p>
