<p><p>The Organic Elemental Analyzer market comprises various types of chromatography techniques. GC (Gas Chromatography) is used for separating and analyzing volatile compounds. Frontal Chromatography involves introducing a sample into a stationary phase, allowing for the analysis of the components based on their interaction with the phase. Adsorption-Desorption focuses on the reversible interaction between analytes and a solid phase, aiding in the assessment of material properties. Each method serves unique applications in elemental analysis and materials science.</p></p>
