<p><p>Email applications can be categorized into two main market types: public cloud and on-premises. Public cloud email solutions are hosted by third-party providers and accessible over the internet, offering scalability, cost-effectiveness, and ease of maintenance. In contrast, on-premises email applications are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more resources for management and maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs and preferences regarding accessibility and data governance.</p></p>
