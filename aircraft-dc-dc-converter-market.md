<p><strong>The Aircraft DC-DC Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isolated DC-DC Converters</li><li>Non-isolated DC-DC Converters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft DC-DC converter market is divided into two main types: isolated and non-isolated DC-DC converters. Isolated DC-DC converters utilize transformer-based designs to separate input and output, enhancing safety and reducing noise, making them ideal for sensitive avionics. Non-isolated DC-DC converters, on the other hand, provide direct voltage conversion without electrical isolation, offering simpler designs and higher efficiency for applications where isolation is unnecessary. Both types cater to the diverse power management needs of modern aircraft systems.</p></p>
