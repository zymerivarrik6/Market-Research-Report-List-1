<p><strong>The Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) enhances vehicle maneuverability by using electric motors to assist steering, improving efficiency and reducing fuel consumption. The market is divided into three types: C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering), which integrates the motor within the steering column for compact design; P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering), where the motor is mounted on the pinion gear for enhanced feedback; and R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering), utilizing a motor on the steering rack for optimal responsiveness and precision.</p></p>
