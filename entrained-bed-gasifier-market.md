<p><ul><li>Fixed Bed</li><li>Fluidized Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The entrained bed gasifier market is characterized by two main types: fixed bed and fluidized bed gasifiers. Fixed bed gasifiers operate with a stationary bed of feedstock, allowing for higher carbon conversion rates but are limited by scalability and feedstock size variability. In contrast, fluidized bed gasifiers use a moving bed of particulate material, providing better heat transfer and uniformity, thus allowing for greater flexibility in feedstock types and sizes. Each type has distinct advantages suited for different applications.</p></p>
