<p><p>The mobile ticketing system market can be categorized into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises systems are hosted locally within an organization’s infrastructure, offering greater control and security but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems operate on external servers accessed via the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower costs due to reduced hardware requirements. This allows organizations to quickly adapt to changing demands and enhances user accessibility across various devices.</p></p>
