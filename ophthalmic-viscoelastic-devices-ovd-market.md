<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Viscoelastic Devices OVD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersives OVDs</li><li>Cohesive OVDs</li><li>Combination OVDs</li><li>Visco-Adapative OVDs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic viscoelastic device (OVD) market includes four main types: </p><p>1. **Dispersive OVDs**: These provide a thin, protective layer on the corneal endothelium, ideal for maintaining space during cataract surgery. </p><p>2. **Cohesive OVDs**: Offering high viscosity, they effectively maintain space and protect intraocular tissues during surgical procedures.</p><p>3. **Combination OVDs**: These utilize properties from both dispersive and cohesive types, optimizing performance for various surgical needs.</p><p>4. **Visco-Adaptive OVDs**: These adjust their viscosity based on surgical conditions, providing versatility and enhanced efficacy during procedures.</p></p>
