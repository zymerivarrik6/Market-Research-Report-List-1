<p><p>The construction additives market encompasses three primary types: chemical, mineral, and fiber additives. Chemical additives enhance properties such as workability, durability, and setting time. Mineral additives, including silica fume and fly ash, improve strength and reduce permeability, contributing to sustainability. Fiber additives, such as steel, glass, or synthetic fibers, reinforce composite materials, reducing cracking and increasing impact resistance. Collectively, these additives play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and longevity of construction materials.</p></p>
