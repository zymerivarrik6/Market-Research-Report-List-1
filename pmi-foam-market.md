<p><p>The PMI foam market is segmented into three primary types: standard, fireproof, and radio proof. Standard PMI foam is widely used for insulation and lightweight structural components due to its excellent strength-to-weight ratio. Fireproof PMI foam incorporates additives to enhance its fire resistance, making it suitable for applications in aerospace and automotive sectors where safety is critical. Radio proof PMI foam is designed to block or attenuate electromagnetic radiation, making it ideal for shielding in communication technologies and sensitive electronic equipment.</p></p>
