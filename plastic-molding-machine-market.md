<p><p>The plastic molding machine market encompasses various types, each tailored for specific manufacturing processes. Forming molding shapes materials by applying heat and pressure, while injection molding forces molten plastic into molds for precise shapes. Extrusion molding continuously shapes plastic by forcing it through a die, ideal for producing long sections like pipes. Pultrusion molding involves pulling reinforced fibers through a resin bath and a heated die to create strong, lightweight profiles, commonly used in construction and automotive applications.</p></p>
