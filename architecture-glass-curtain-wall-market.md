<p><p>The architectural glass curtain wall market encompasses various types, each offering distinct features. Frame support curtain walls rely on a structural framework, providing durability while allowing for large glass panels. All-glass curtain walls prioritize aesthetics, featuring minimal framing for a sleek look. Point support curtain walls utilize discrete supports at specific points, enhancing design flexibility. Unit-typed curtain walls consist of pre-fabricated panels, expediting installation and ensuring uniformity. Each type caters to different design preferences, structural requirements, and construction methods in modern architecture.</p></p>
