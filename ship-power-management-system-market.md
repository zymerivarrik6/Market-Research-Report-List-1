<p><p>The Ship Power Management System (PMS) market comprises two primary types: Power Generation Systems and Power Distribution Systems. Power Generation Systems focus on producing electricity aboard ships through various sources, such as diesel generators and renewable energy. In contrast, Power Distribution Systems manage the distribution of generated power to various ship systems, ensuring efficient and reliable energy flow. Both types are essential for optimizing energy consumption, enhancing operational efficiency, improving safety, and supporting the increasing reliance on electrification in maritime operations.</p></p>
