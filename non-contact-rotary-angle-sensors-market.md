<p><p>Non-contact rotary angle sensors utilize magnetic or optical technologies to measure angular position without physical contact, ensuring high precision and longevity. Single output sensors provide one voltage or digital signal corresponding to the angle, ideal for basic applications. In contrast, dual output sensors offer two independent signals, enhancing redundancy and reliability, making them suitable for critical applications like robotics and automotive systems. The choice between single and dual outputs depends on the required accuracy, safety, and application complexity.</p></p>
