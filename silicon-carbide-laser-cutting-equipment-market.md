<p><p>Silicon carbide laser cutting equipment primarily includes CO2 and fiber laser cutting technologies. CO2 laser cutting utilizes a gas mixture to produce high-energy beams, ideal for cutting thick materials and providing smooth edges. In contrast, fiber laser cutting employs a solid-state laser that delivers higher efficiency and faster processing speeds, making it suitable for thinner materials and intricate designs. Each technology offers unique advantages, influencing its application across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics, where precision cutting is essential.</p></p>
