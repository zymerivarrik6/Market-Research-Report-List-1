<p><ul><li>Mono-split</li><li>Multi-split</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The split air conditioning market is primarily divided into two types: mono-split and multi-split systems. Mono-split systems consist of one indoor unit connected to a single outdoor condenser, making them ideal for single rooms or small spaces. In contrast, multi-split systems connect multiple indoor units to one outdoor unit, allowing for temperature control across different rooms or zones. This flexibility enables users to tailor cooling solutions to their specific needs, enhancing energy efficiency and comfort in larger areas.</p></p>
