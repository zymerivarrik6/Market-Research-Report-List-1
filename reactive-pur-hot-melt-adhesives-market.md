<p><p>Reactive PUR hot-melt adhesives are categorized into two market types: Type I and Type II. Type I adhesives are primarily used for bonding porous substrates, offering excellent flexibility and durability. They are ideal for applications in furniture and wood assembly. Type II adhesives, on the other hand, are designed for more demanding applications where high moisture resistance and thermal stability are required, making them suitable for laminating and automotive industries. Both types leverage the benefits of reactive polyurethane chemistry for strong, lasting bonds.</p></p>
