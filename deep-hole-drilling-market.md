<p><p>The Deep Hole Drilling Market comprises various methods, including Gun Drilling, BTA (Boring and Trepanning Association), and STS (Single Tube System). Gun Drilling is a precise technique suitable for creating deep, straight holes with minimal diameter. BTA is designed for larger diameters, using a single tool for both drilling and chip removal, ideal for high-volume production. STS, a variant of BTA, utilizes a single tube system for efficient chip removal. Together, these methods cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing precision and efficiency in deep drilling operations.</p></p>
