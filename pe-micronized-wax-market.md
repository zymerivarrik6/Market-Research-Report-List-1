<p><p>The PE Micronized Wax market encompasses two primary types: Polyethylene Waxes and Oxidized Polyethylene Waxes. Polyethylene Waxes are low molecular weight polyethylene compounds that enhance properties like slip, gloss, and durability in coatings, inks, and plastics. In contrast, Oxidized Polyethylene Waxes have undergone a modification process, resulting in improved adhesion and wettability, making them ideal for applications in adhesives and sealants. Both types are valued for their versatility and ability to improve the performance of various formulations.</p></p>
