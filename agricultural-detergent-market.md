<p><p>The agricultural detergent market is categorized into three main types: acid, neutral, and alkaline detergents. Acid detergents are typically used for removing mineral deposits and rust, making them effective for cleaning equipment and surfaces exposed to hard water. Neutral detergents are versatile and suitable for general cleaning tasks, providing effective removal of organic matter without damaging surfaces. Alkaline detergents excel in breaking down grease, oils, and fats, making them ideal for heavy-duty cleaning in agricultural settings, enhancing equipment performance and cleanliness.</p></p>
