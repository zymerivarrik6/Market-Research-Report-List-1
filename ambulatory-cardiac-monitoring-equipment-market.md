<p><ul><li>Holter Monitor</li><li>Event Monitors</li><li>Mobile Cardiac Telemetry</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ambulatory cardiac monitoring equipment market encompasses several types. Holter monitors are portable devices that continuously record cardiac activity for 24-48 hours. Event monitors are used to capture heart rhythms during specific occurrences of symptoms, often activated by the patient. Mobile cardiac telemetry provides real-time monitoring and immediate transmission of data to healthcare providers. Other market types may include wearable devices or implantable loop recorders, which offer varied monitoring durations and data analysis capabilities, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
