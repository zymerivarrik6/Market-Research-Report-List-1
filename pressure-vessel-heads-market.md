<p><p>Pressure vessel heads are critical components designed to withstand internal pressure and contribute to the overall strength of vessels. Flat heads are simple and economical, suitable for low-pressure applications. Hemispherical heads enhance strength and are efficient for high-pressure systems. Ellipsoidal heads provide a balance between strength and volume, ideal for various pressures. Torispherical heads combine flat and curved shapes, offering versatility. Other types may include conical or dome-shaped heads, catering to specialized requirements in different industries.</p></p>
